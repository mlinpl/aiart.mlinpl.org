Is our Festival mentioned in the podcast? Yes!

we encourage you to listen to the latest episode of the Wider AI Context podcast entitled AI instead of a paintbrush. How artists are using technology. The two judges of our competition: Jakub Depczyński - art historian and Przemysław Spurek, Ph.D. - leader of the research team at IDEAS NCBR talk about what surprised them in the submitted works, how the visual culture of society is shaping up, and how artists are exploring the use of a tool such as artificial intelligence in the creative process. Great 45 minutes of conversation!
