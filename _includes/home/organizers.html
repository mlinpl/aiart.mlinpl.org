/ Organizers

The festival is organized by the non-profit ML in PL Association in collaboration with the Museum of Modern Art in Warsaw and the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw.

ML in PL Association

We are a group of young people who are determined to bring the best of Machine Learning to Central and Eastern Europe by creating a high-quality event for every ML and AI enthusiast. Although we come from many different backgrounds, we are united by the common goal of spreading a love about the discipline.

Museum of Modern Art in Warsaw

The Museum of Modern Art in Warsaw (Muzeum Sztuki Nowoczesnej w Warszawie, or MSN) is a leading cultural institution in Poland focused on contemporary and modern art. MSN collaborates with various groups, communities, and institutions both in Poland and internationally, serving as a hub for dialogue and shaping the cognitive and aesthetic sensibilities.

Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw

With a heritage spanning over 250 years, the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw stands as the largest and oldest institution of higher education for the arts in Poland. The Academy upholds the traditions and values of Polish art, emphasizing openness, freedom in artistic exploration and scientific research, and respect for diverse creative perspectives.
