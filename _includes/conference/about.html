/ Competition

This year competition is titled "Future will be better Tommorow". In this competition we encourage all the artists, students, AI enthusiasts - from all fields and all backgrounds - to create their own artworks elaborating on the topic of future of AI and our society, while using AI in the creative process. Finalists have a chance to present their work on the exhibition in Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw. Cash prizes await for top 3 artworks.
